**Serendib Yatra** is a visually stunning and comprehensive tourism website dedicated to showcasing the pearl of the Indian Ocean—Sri Lanka. From ancient history to breathtaking landscapes, this platform acts as a digital guide for travelers looking to explore the island's beauty.

🌐 **Visit Website:** [https://srilanka.whitecoder.online/](https://srilanka.whitecoder.online/)

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 🗺️ **Explore Destinations:** Discover popular tourist hotspots such as Sigiriya, Kandy, Ella, Mirissa, and Colombo with detailed guides and high-quality imagery.
* 🏛️ **History & Heritage:** Learn about Sri Lanka's rich history, including UNESCO World Heritage Sites like the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya.
* 🐘 **Experiences & Adventures:** Categorized sections for varied travel experiences, including Wildlife Safaris (Yala, Minneriya), Thrilling Adventures (Surfing, Rafting), Cultural Festivals, and Wellness retreats.
* 📱 **Responsive Design:** A fully responsive, modern UI with smooth scrolling and beautiful image galleries that work perfectly on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
* 📰 **Travel Blogs & Info:** Features sections for travel tips, blogs, and essential information for tourists planning their journey to Sri Lanka.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. **Discover:** Users land on an engaging hero section and can explore top destinations via the navigation menu or popular tags.
2. **Read Details:** Clicking on a featured destination (e.g., Sigiriya) reveals its history, best times to visit, things to do, and a photo gallery.
3. **Find Experiences:** Users can browse through experience cards (Culture, Thrills, Wildlife, Wellness, Gastronomy) to find activities that match their interests.
4. **Plan the Trip:** The footer provides quick links to travel info, visa requirements, and newsletters to help users plan their visit.
